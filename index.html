Apeiro

Not only a frontier academic community but also a bridge between academic research and industry

ABOUT US

Our Vision

Apeiro aims to build the AI community and provide a platform for world-class AI researchers to communicate and explore freely

Research Areas

Apeiro are exploring the interdisciplinary field of artificial intelligence, such as computational game theory and quantum artificial intelligence algorithms

Current Focus

Build an advanced AI platform that specializes in constructing, visualizing, and learning massive (dynamic) relational data

Things We Do

Relational Database

Relational database construction is one of the essential components of Apeiro’s AI platform. The platform’s natural language processing modules can provide enterprises domain-specific enterprise-level knowledge graph. Advanced algorithms for analyzing, visualizing, and learning the relational database are developed to fulfill the user-specific demands.

Fundamental Research

Fundamental research related to machine learning and artificial intelligence is also on Apeiro’s agenda. Apeiro aims to publish high-quality academic papers in top international conferences and journals, create high-tech intellectual property rights. The cutting-edge fundamental research of AI can facilitate the cultivation of high-end talents in AI.

Education

Apeiro holds scientific lectures and summer schools in different fields such as AI, machine learning, computation game theory, etc., for students across the country, and set up online education courses to build a research community.

Cooperation

Apeiro conducts academic cooperation with universities and research institutions in China and abroad, introduce talents, teams, and projects; promote talent exchange.
